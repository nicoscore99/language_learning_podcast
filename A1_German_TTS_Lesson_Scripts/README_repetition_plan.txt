TTS format:
Each lesson is a plain .txt file containing only spoken lesson text, <lang code="en" />, <lang code="de" />, <lang code="auto" />, and <break time="...s" /> tags.

Pause rule:
Pauses are deliberately longer than the first version. Response pauses are usually 3.5 to 8.5 seconds, and reflection pauses after model answers are usually 3.5 to 6.0 seconds, so the learner has more time to process and repeat.

Repetition strategy:
Each lesson introduces beginner vocabulary, gives clearer English explanations of German words and the main verb focus, practises one main verb or phrase pattern, includes short sentence practice, revisits earlier material starting in lesson 2, and ends with final recall.

Adaptation note:
The original attached prompt was for Mandarin-speaking learners of English. These regenerated files adapt the structure for English-speaking learners of German at A1 level.
