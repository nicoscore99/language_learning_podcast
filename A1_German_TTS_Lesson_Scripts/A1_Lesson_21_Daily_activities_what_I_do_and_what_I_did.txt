The main language focus today is the verb "to do or to make". "to do or to make" in German is: "machen". You will practise these forms: ich mache, du machst, er macht, sie macht, wir machen, ich habe gemacht.
<break time="5.0s" />

<lang code="auto" />
A small grammar point for today: A first simple past pattern is “ich habe gemacht”. It means “I did” or “I have done”.
