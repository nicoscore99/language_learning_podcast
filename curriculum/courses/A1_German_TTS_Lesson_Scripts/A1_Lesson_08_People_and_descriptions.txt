The main language focus today is the verb "to be". "to be" in German is: "sein". You will practise these forms: er ist, sie ist, das ist.

<break time="5.0s" />

<lang code="auto" />
A small grammar point for today: Use “er ist” for he is and “sie ist” for she is. “Das ist” means “that is” or “this is”.
