Welcome. This is German A1 lesson 19. The topic is Objects and gender. You will hear English prompts, and you should answer in German.

<break time="3.0s" />

<lang code="auto" />
Today you will learn these German words and expressions: Tisch, Stuhl, Tasche, Handy, Buch, haben.

<break time="4.0s" />

<lang code="auto" />
The main language focus today is the verb "to have". "to have" in German is: "haben". You will practise these forms: ich habe einen, ich habe eine, ich habe ein.

<break time="5.0s" />

<lang code="auto" />
A small grammar point for today: German nouns have gender. Use “einen” for many masculine objects, “eine” for feminine objects, and “ein” for neuter objects after “haben”.
